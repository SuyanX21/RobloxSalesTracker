Roblox Sales Tracker Extension

This browser extension helps you track your Roblox sales and revenue in a simple, easy-to-read popup. It displays your sales for today, the past 7 days, and all time, along with estimated Robux earned.

Reminder: This project is fully open source & safe (you can download the plugin files from Chrome using Extension Source Viewer )

How It Works

  • Automatically fetches your sales data from Roblox (when logged in).
  • Shows sales and Robux estimates in a compact popup UI.
  • Lets you reset the tracker if needed.

Is My Data Safe?

Your data is only used locally in your browser. The extension does not send your information anywhere else.
